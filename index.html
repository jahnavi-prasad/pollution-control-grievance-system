Background of Innovation

As part of IBM- 2020 Call for Code Global Challange
I have selected track 2 : Climate change

To effectively battle climate change,
we need to focus on its biggest cause,
Man-made Pollution.
We need to root out this menace at minute level
and thus the idea of a grievence portal to complain and take action
about causes of pollution was born.
PCGS is like a cross between Change.org and facebook.com
Here users can post pictures and location of causes of pollution near them,
other users can upvote existing issues that they think needs resolving,
all users collectively urge Pollution Control Board to solve these issues.

Problem statement

Every day we come across several sources of pollution, polluting the very basics of our lives Water, Land, Air and Noise. Citizens do not have idea about the who/where to report it and how. Some cities and state governments have grievance portals, but every citizen may not have enough time and interest to undergo the tedious and tiresome process of complaining about the issue, then keeping track of the progress and making sure that certain steps are taken to stop the issue from repeating. So, a centralized portal that can be accessed by everyone in the country to report, support, act and verify on the issue is much needed.

About PCGS.
